Detailed Description

Position-level and velocity-level contact constraint solvers.

All functions operate on ContactList (the unified SoA defined in CollisionPipeline.hpp). Naming follows the XPBD literature:

  • λ (lambda) — accumulated Lagrange multiplier (constraint impulse magnitude).
  • Δλ (delta_lambda) — per-iteration increment.
  • w — generalised inverse mass along the constraint direction.
